Description

We are a small yet successful independent studio from Poland. Our team has created and supported titles such as #DRIVE and Ski Jump which boast 25 million players, numerous nominations, and awards, including the title of "Game of the Year." Pixel Perfect Dude always strives to deliver high-quality titles for all platforms.
