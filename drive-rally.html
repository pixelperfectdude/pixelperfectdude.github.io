Description

#DRIVE Rally lets you relive the 90s in the most exciting era in motorsport history, where dust, mud, and snow are badges of honor. Unleash the raw power of legendary rally beasts, admire every second of the stunning landscapes, and remember: the faster you go, the better! And not only it nails the spirit of the 90s. It reignites it for a new generation of drivers. Whether you're a veteran of dirt roads or a rookie just looking to get their tires wet, this game will let you redefine speed over and over again.

Features

  • #DRIVE World: Explore diverse landscapes like Dry Crumbs, Holzberg, and Revontuli.
  • Legendary Cars: Drive iconic rally cars such as Das Holzwagen, The Doggo, and The Bobond, with new additions like Das Sandsturm and Celestia from the 90s.
  • Passionate Co-Drivers: Partner with co-drivers who offer support and critique, celebrating victories and facing failures together.
  • #DRIVE Design: Customize cars from scratch, including body, paintwork, decals, and decorations.
  • #DRIVE Arcade Mechanics: Balances simplicity for beginners with depth for experienced players, bringing joy back to racing games.
  • Retro Revival: Packed with throwbacks and Easter eggs for car enthusiasts to reminisce.
  • Photo Mode: Capture and customize your best moments, then share them with friends.

About Pixel Perfect Dude

We are a small yet successful independent studio from Poland. Our team has created and supported titles such as #DRIVE and Ski Jump which boast 25 million players, numerous nominations, and awards, including the title of "Game of the Year." Pixel Perfect Dude always strives to deliver high-quality titles for all platforms.
